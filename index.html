Privacy Policy

QR Code Scanner

Last updated: 2026

Overview

QR Code Scanner is a utility application that allows users to scan and generate QR codes and barcodes, scan documents, convert images to PDF, and manage files locally on their device. Your privacy is very important to us.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All features work entirely on your device.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes, barcodes, and documents.
  • Storage / Media Access: Used to save generated QR codes, scanned documents, and PDFs.
  • Vibration: Used to provide feedback when a scan is completed.

Data Storage

All scanned and generated data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload, sync, or back up your files to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

QR Code Scanner does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app store listing.
